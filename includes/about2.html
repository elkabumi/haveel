Haveel High Pressure Decorative Laminates comprise layer of specially selected decorative papers, impegnated with melamine and phenolic resins, and kraft paper, which is pressed together under temperatures of 120 °C (± 5 °C) and high pressure of 100 kg (± 5 kg) per cm².
These are high-quality, hard-wearing and impact-resistant laminates, and provide a beautiful decorative finish. Applications of this material include kitchen cabinet, office furniture, counter-tops, store fixtures and other domestic and commercial application.

Laminate surface can be cleaned using the following method :
  • Light dirt of stains can be removed simply with warm soapy water or a mixture warm water and detergent.
  • Grease marks and adhesive residue can be removed with white mineral spirits.
  • Strong colored fruit, hair dues, vegetables juices, bleaches, caustic-alkalis and strong mineral acids may leave a mark if not wiped off within a short period.
  • Stubborn dirt and stains may be removed using non-abrasive scouring cream or mild domestic bleach.
  • Harsh abrasive should not be used.
